About MysterFlags

This simple flag quiz was made by Jo7ico (who loves geopolitics) to help people memorize flags in a fun way.

Jo7ico is not taking credit of any of the images (except for logo) used in this website.

Why use MysterFlags?

MysterFlags flag quiz includes all countries/states recognized by the UN and non-official countries such as the Republic of China (Taiwan) and Palestine. Users have the option to guess flags in a continent they can choose (or the whole world). Users may guess up to 50 flags.

MysterFlags flag quiz is completely free, unlimited, and doesn't require sign-ups.
